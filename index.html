About this Map
This map shows residential buildings of 5 or more units in Queens Community Districts 1 and 2 that are either under consuction or were completed since 2016. The data source is the NYC Department of City Planning's Housing Database, which relies on Department of Buildings information and is updated semi-annually.

Hover over a circle to see information about the building.
